Local slice of heaven

Newport locals' surf spot
Date: May 9th, 2023

The Wedge is known for its massive waves that can reach up to 30 feet high, attracting surfers and bodyboarders from around the world. The best time to catch the Wedge is during a south swell, especially in the summer and early fall when the water is warmer and the crowds are at their peak. However, the Wedge is not for the faint of heart, as the waves can be incredibly dangerous, with strong currents and a shallow sandy bottom.

Despite its dangers, the Wedge is a beloved spot for many surfers who seek the thrill of catching the biggest waves. It's not uncommon to see professional surfers and bodyboarders taking on the Wedge's massive walls of water, performing jaw-dropping tricks and maneuvers that leave onlookers in awe. But even for the most experienced surfers, the Wedge demands respect and caution, as one wrong move can result in serious injury or worse.

The Wedge is a surf spot like no other, where adrenaline junkies come to test their skills and courage against some of the largest and most powerful waves on the planet. While it may not be suitable for beginners, experienced surfers and bodyboarders will find the Wedge to be an unforgettable experience that will keep them coming back for more. So if you're ever in Newport Beach, be sure to check out the Wedge and witness the raw power of the ocean firsthand.

Written by Greg Peter Thomas

Hidden by the moonlight

A hidden surf spot found in Half Moon Bay, with an even bigger surprise after midnight.

Half Moon Bay is a well-known destination for surfers and beach-goers alike. However, there is a hidden gem in this coastal town that only a select few know about - a surf spot where bioluminescent waves can be seen after midnight.

Located off the beaten path, this surf spot is accessible only by foot, and it requires a bit of a trek down a steep and rocky path. But for those who are brave enough to make the journey, the reward is a stunning and otherworldly experience that is truly unforgettable. The bioluminescent waves can only be seen after midnight when the moon is at its darkest. The waves glow with a stunning blue-green hue, creating a breathtaking display of light and color that is mesmerizing to watch.

Surfers who are lucky enough to catch a wave under these conditions describe it as a surreal experience. The waves light up as they ride them, leaving a glowing trail of light behind them. It's a truly magical moment that few people get to experience.

One surfer, who prefers to remain anonymous, says that catching a wave under the bioluminescent conditions is one of the most incredible experiences of his life. "It's like surfing in a dream," he says. "The waves light up beneath you, and you feel like you're gliding on a river of stars. It's hard to describe in words - you just have to experience it for yourself."

While the surf spot is relatively unknown and not easy to get to, surfers who have discovered it keep coming back for more. It's a truly unique and unforgettable experience that is not to be missed.

However, it's important to remember that this surf spot is located in a sensitive ecosystem, and surfers are urged to respect the environment and the wildlife that call it home. It's also essential to ensure that you are an experienced surfer who can handle the conditions safely.

So if you're looking for a truly unforgettable surf experience, head down to Half Moon Bay and discover the hidden surf spot where bioluminescent waves light up the night sky. Just remember to bring your sense of adventure and a healthy respect for the natural environment.

Written by Greg Peter Thomas

Cold Spring Tavern

Nestled in the scenic Santa Ynez Mountains of Southern California, Cold Spring Tavern is the perfect rest stop for cyclists embarking on a challenging bike packing adventure. With its rich history dating back to the 1800s, the tavern offers a unique and rustic atmosphere, delicious food and drink, and breathtaking views of the surrounding wilderness that will recharge any weary traveler's spirits.
