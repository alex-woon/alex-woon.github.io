Cold Spring Tavern

Nestled in the scenic Santa Ynez Mountains of Southern California, Cold Spring Tavern is the perfect rest stop for cyclists embarking on a challenging bike packing adventure. With its rich history dating back to the 1800s, the tavern offers a unique and rustic atmosphere, delicious food and drink, and breathtaking views of the surrounding wilderness that will recharge any weary traveler's spirits.
